How do I use my WRAP coins ?

The Wrap coins can be used to redeem swags or rewards from our platform. Also Wrap coins can be used buys products, discount coupons and recipts from associates. Using Wrap coins exclusive products can be prebooked the products of company that we have colabrated.

Do I earn coins by refering friends to WRAP?

Yes, you earn 20 Wrap coins per friend that you invite to Wrap. Once your friend install the Wrap app, you will receive invitation reward. When the invited person initiates his first Wrap pickup using the app, then you will recieve 50 Wrap coins as reward.

Where does the collected waste go ?

Through door to door pickup the sorted E-waste,plastic and other recycleable wastes are collected by autherised authorities. The collected waste is then taken to the collection centers and once again properly sorted into E-waste,recycleable waste and reuseable waste. Then the recycleable wastes are sent to recycling factories and resuseable wastes are sent to proper factories for further processing.
